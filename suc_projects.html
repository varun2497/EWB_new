Are you aware that in India out of 1.3 billion people still lack access to clean water, 75% lack adequate sanitation and 25% live below poverty levels? Taking into account the intense need for a solution in these fields, we have currently taken up 2 projects:

Sanitation

Our aim is to target the village outskirts of Chennai, with around 900 families. Major stakeholders will be daily wage workers and florists with an average income of Rs. 5000 per month. These families do not have an access to toilet facilities and resort to open defecation. The community population will be a direct beneficiary of this project.

View Project

Solar Electrification at Government Schools

More than 1.4 billion people worldwide have no access to electricity. In India, of the 80 million homes in the country, 32.8% households are off-grid. Another 30-40 million homes are near off-grid. It is common for black-outs to last for 10-12 hours a day. Despite great advances in solar photovoltaic technology over the past half-century, it has not been widely adopted for electricity generation.
